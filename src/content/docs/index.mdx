## What is SimplyAds?

SimplyAds is a comprehensive, bilingual Google Ads dictionary designed to make digital advertising accessible to everyone. Whether you're a marketing professional, small business owner, or just getting started with online advertising, our simplified definitions and real-world examples will help you master Google Ads terminology.

## Key Categories

Our dictionary covers essential Google Ads concepts across these main areas:

- **Metrics & KPIs**: [CPC](/metrics/cpc/), [CTR](/metrics/ctr/), [ROAS](/metrics/roas/), [conversion rates](/metrics/conversion-rate/), and [performance indicators](/optimization/kpi/)
- **Bidding & Budget**: [Automated bidding](/bidding-budget/automated-bidding/), [CPC max](/bidding-budget/cpc-max/), [daily budgets](/bidding-budget/daily-budget/), and cost optimization  
- **Targeting**: [Keywords](/targeting/keyword/), [match types](/targeting/match-type/), [audiences](/targeting/audience/), and [demographic targeting](/targeting/geolocation/)
- **Campaign Types**: [Search](/formats-networks/search-network/), [Display](/formats-networks/display-network/), [Shopping](/formats-networks/shopping-ad/), Video, and App campaigns
- **Optimization**: [Quality Score](/metrics/ad-quality/), [ad extensions](/technical/ad-extensions/), landing page experience
