### Definition
Cost per acquisition/[conversion](/metrics/conversion).

### Formula
```

CPA = Total Ad Spend ÷ Number of Conversions
```

### Example
€50 spent for 5 sales → CPA = €10.

<Aside type="tip">Your target CPA should be lower than your average customer lifetime value or profit per sale.</Aside>

<Aside type="note">CPA can vary significantly by conversion type (purchase vs. lead) and should be evaluated against business value</Aside>
