### Definition
Return on advertising spend.

### Formula
```
ROAS = Revenue from Ads ÷ Cost of Ads
```

### Example
€100 invested → €500 in sales → ROAS = 5.

<Aside type="tip">A ROAS of 4:1 or higher is generally considered good, but this varies by industry and profit margins.</Aside>

<Aside type="note">ROAS focuses on revenue, not profit. Consider your profit margins when evaluating ROAS performance.</Aside>
