### Definition
Average cost paid for a click on an ad.

### Formula
```
CPC = Total Cost ÷ Total Clicks
```

### Example
100 clicks cost €50, CPC = €0.50.

<Aside type="tip">Lower CPC generally means more efficient ad spending, but should be balanced with conversion quality.</Aside>

<Aside type="caution">Very low CPC might indicate poor ad targeting or low-quality traffic that doesn't convert.</Aside>
